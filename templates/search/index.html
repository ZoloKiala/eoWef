What is EO-WEF ?

The EO-WEF (Earth Observations for water, energy, food nexus) app is a multisectoral information system that generates data in a time series format for different components of a generic nexus system using earth observations and other data sources (e.g.climate, population, etc.). The app is powered by Google Earth Engine and Tethys platform

The tool is designed to help people who do not have prior knowledge on remote sensing and programming to create customizable data that are the result of on-demand processing from Google Earth Engine. Unlike similar web applications that have been developed over the last decade, the EO-WEF app is mobile responsive and is developed with the wef nexus approach in mind.

Motivation

Earth observations have the potential to provide datasets for the different components of a generic nexus system. Although many tools exist that generate earth observation datasets, most of them are generally limited to specific components of a generic nexus system. To the best of our knowledge, there is none that structures them into a queryable table for the entire nexus and allow their visualization and the extraction of their timeseries.

Further development
  • Adding more datasets from the Google Earth Engine data catalogue.
  • Allow users to download displayed images and data drawn on timeseries graphs in a csv format.
  • Enable comparison of variables.
