How to use the EO-WEF ?

The EO-WEF app is subdivided into 2 parts: the search and map sections.

In the search section,the following steps can be used:

1
Use the search bar to look up in the EO-WEF database the variable of interest and its sector, satellite (or model) and product and the availability dates of its data
2
Click the product link(s) to learn about the data associated to the selected variable.
3
Record the information related to the variable. This will help to visualize its data and time series on the map area.

The map section of the application consists of visualizing the data of the selected variable and retrieving the time series.

The steps are as follows:

4
Enter the recorded information related to the selected variable.
5
Choose the reducer method (e.g. median) to display the image collection for a date range, which should be within the availability dates of the variable data.
6
Click “load” to display the reduced image collection on the map view area. In contrast, the “clear” button removes the displayed image.
7
To plot the time series of the variable data for the selected date range at a location, use the draw tool menu on the map view area, drop a point or draw a polygon and click the “Plot AOI” button.
8
To customize the visualization of images to a specific area, use the “set boundary” button to upload your own shapefile.
